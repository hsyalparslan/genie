Desirable

I wish the sun would shine day long,
So I could just store energy,
Like composing a rhyme
That would be glorious.

I wish the best chorus could be,
Automatically singing song for me,
All the day, tinkling in ears.
Every minute, every second

I wish I could hold an amazing party,
For people who could dance during the all night,
Madly, passionately, ambitiously.
Without thinking any sorrow or grief.
