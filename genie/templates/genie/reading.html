Reading

In this particular activity, read these short texts. Be a group which consist of 3 people. Do these two activities with your group friends.

1st activity: Choose one of them. Then describe the world of the person that you choose. You can describe it whatever you want like by drawing, speaking or writing, etc.

2nd activity: Create your dream world with your group friends. Tell what would you wish that what kind of world you want to live.
